───── BIOLOGICAL ARCHITECTURE ─────

Levitation Mechanism: Achieved via magical means . The internal core generates extreme heat through rapid biomass decomposition, inflating a network of reinforced ventral bladders with hydrogen-rich gas. Peripheral "tentacle-rudders" provide directional thrust by manipulating high-altitude wind currents.
The Orbital Debris Halo: A kinetic shield composed of magnetized scrap metal, pulverized concrete, and calcified bone. This 400-meter field rotates at high velocity, acting as an automated interceptor for incoming missiles. Projectiles are either shredded by the debris or deflected by the entity's localized electromagnetic pulse.
Atmospheric Re-Conditioning: The Ascendant functions as a mobile terraforming unit. It continuously filters oxygen into the "Black Fog" pathogen, creating a 5-mile radius of unbreathable, infectious air beneath its flight path.

───── THE TRINITY CORES (Primary Targets) ─────

!! HIGH VALUE TARGET // NEURAL RELAY !! CORE 01, 02, & 03: THE OCULI

Located in a triangular formation on the ventral (underside) chassis. Each core is protected by a 4-foot thick layer of regenerative chitinous plating.

  • The Gaze Effect: The cores emit a concentrated violet beam (Bio-Photonic Radiation). Contact with this beam causes immediate neural failure and cellular liquefaction in living targets.
  • The Respiration Cycle: To vent excess heat, the chitinous plates retract every 180 seconds. This reveals the "Nerve-Root"—the only point susceptible to small-arms or high-velocity piercing rounds.
  • Hive-Mind Severance: Destroying a single core reduces the entity's mobility by 30%. Destroying all three causes a total buoyancy failure and Hive-Mind blackout.

───── DEFENSIVE & OFFENSIVE SYSTEMS ─────

The Sweeper Tendrils

Miles-long, whip-like appendages designed to "sieve" city streets. They are sensitive to heat and movement, capable of snatching armored vehicles and retracting them into the Intake Maw for biomass processing.

Screamer Cysts

Localized biological turrets embedded in the shell. They fire high-velocity bone shards at aircraft. If these shards pierce a cockpit, the internal pathogen infects the pilot within seconds.
