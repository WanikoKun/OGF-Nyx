───── PHYSICAL PROFILE ─────

Base Appearance:Indistinguishable from original host during incubation.

Known Physical Anomalies:Total lack of pulse; the heart is liquefied during the Restructure to form a central, high-pressure core in the chest cavity.

───── ABILITY CLASSIFICATION ─────

Neural Overwrite:The infection accesses the host’s hippocampus, allowing the entity to use host memories for advanced mimicry, password retrieval, or psychological manipulation of survivors.
