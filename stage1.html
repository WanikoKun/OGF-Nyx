───── ENTITY IDENTIFICATION ─────

Name:The Blight
Designation ID:8187|JJ817W|BCBOHU
Species / Classification:Neurological Parasite / Infection
Origin Status:The Nothing

───── PHYSICAL PROFILE ─────

Base Appearance:Indistinguishable from original host.
Phase A (Silent):Pupils fail to react to light; micro-tremors in the jaw and neck.
Phase B (The Restructure):Formerly known as the "Snap Point." Violent finalization of Stage 1. Host temperature spikes to 106°F. Skeletal system is pulverized and re-set. Rib cages are often inverted to act as external "teeth" or defensive ribbing.
Known Physical Anomalies:Total lack of pulse; the heart is liquefied during the Restructure to form a central "Blight Core" in the chest cavity.

───── ABILITY CLASSIFICATION ─────

Neural Overwrite:The infection uses the host’s own memories to manipulate survivors or bypass security (mimicry).
Adrenaline Siphon:Feeds on human cruelty/fear to accelerate bone growth.

[STAGE 2 SPECIALIZATIONS]

TYPE-S (STALKER) Optimized for stealth. These units retain a human silhouette but can dislocate every joint to move through vents or crawlspaces. They emit a "false distress" vocalization to lure targets.
TYPE-H (HEAVY/BRUTE) Formed from multiple hosts fused together or a single high-biomass host. Bone density is doubled, creating a natural armor plating that is resistant to small-arms fire.
TYPE-C (CASTER/SPORE-BEARER) Non-combat focused. These units remain stationary and act as biological vents, pumping "Nothing" spores into the air to begin the environmental terraforming of Stage 2.
TYPE-V (VANGUARD) High-speed combatants with sharpened radius/ulna bones extending from the forearms as 12-inch blades.

───── FEEDING & SUSTENANCE ─────

Feeding Type:Corruptive / Emotional
Primary Sustenance Source:Human Cruelty
Behavioral Effects of Starvation:If deprived of "cruelty," the entity becomes frantic and "noisy," losing its ability to mimic humans effectively.

───── BEHAVIORAL PROFILE ─────

Baseline Temperament:Predatory / Hostile
Observed Habits:Frequently returns to "places of importance" from the host's former life to set up ambushes for loved ones/associates.
BUREAU OVERSIGHT NOTES:

Immediate quarantine of any individual exhibiting "Restructure tremors." Do not engage Type-H units without high-caliber or incendiary support.
