───── STRUCTURAL RE-ENGINEERING ─────

Bramble Growth:Black, calcified arterial structures sprout from masonry. These "vines" are actually hardened nerve clusters that respond to vibration. If touched, the Brambles contract, alerting every Stage 1 entity within the Bloom radius.
Atmospheric Saturation:The air is replaced by a thick, copper-smelling haze. This "Sporesphere" acts as an external nervous system for the Blight, allowing the Hive-Mind to track movement via air displacement.
Acoustic Thrumming:Affected structures emit a low-frequency hum (approx. 8Hz). This frequency has been shown to cause nausea, dental pain, and auditory hallucinations in un-infected humans.

[FUNCTIONAL PURPOSE]

TACTICAL ADVISORY:
"Destroy these first. Without a Reclamation Vat, the local Stage 1 units cannot repair themselves and will eventually become brittle and 'dry out'."
